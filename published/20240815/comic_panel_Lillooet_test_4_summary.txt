Wildfire Recovery Efforts in Lillooet
Residents near Lillooet are facing challenges in recovering from the 2023 wildfires due to bureaucratic delays.
Title: "Embers of Hope"

In the aftermath of the 2023 wildfires, residents near Lillooet face an unexpected battle - bureaucratic delays that hinder their recovery efforts. Jane, a determined and frustrated resident, stands in front of her burned down house, clutching a photo of her family. The scorched landscape serves as a stark reminder of what they've lost, but also fuels their determination to rebuild and move forward. This comic panel captures the struggle and resilience of those affected by the wildfires, as they navigate through the challenges of recovery and find hope amidst the ashes.
